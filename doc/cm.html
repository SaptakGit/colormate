Our Services

Every paint job, done right

From a single bedroom to a full residential tower — Color Mate brings expertise, certified painters and premium materials to every project in Kolkata.

🎨

Interior Painting

Transform your living spaces using Asian Paints Royale, Berger Silk and Dulux Velvet Touch. Flawless, lasting finishes on every surface in your Kolkata home.

  • Living rooms, bedrooms, kitchen & bathrooms
  • Ceiling painting with anti-fungal primer
  • Feature walls & accent finishes
  • Putty + primer + 2-coat finish as standard
  • Furniture masking & post-work cleanup included
From Rs12/sq ft
🏠

Exterior Painting

Kolkata's climate demands tough exterior protection. We use weather-shield and UV-resistant paints to protect your facade from heavy rain, humidity and sun damage year-round.

  • Weather-resistant exterior emulsions
  • Surface prep, crack-filling & sealing
  • Scaffolding setup included for high-rise buildings
  • Suitable for residential & commercial properties
  • 2-year exterior painting warranty
From Rs18/sq ft
✨

Texture Finish

Add depth and luxury to your walls with our decorative texture finishes. From rustic sand to modern 3D panels — we create walls that become the centrepiece of any room.

  • Sand texture, stucco & rustic plaster
  • Venetian plaster & marble effect
  • 3D geometric & panel textures
  • Metallic & shimmer finishes
  • Feature wall design consultation included
From Rs35/sq ft
💧

Waterproofing

Protect your home from Kolkata's intense monsoons using Dr. Fixit and BASF systems. Permanently seal terraces, bathrooms, basements and walls against water ingress.

  • Terrace & roof waterproofing
  • Bathroom & wet area treatment
  • Basement & underground wall sealing
  • Crack injection & structural repair
  • 5-year material warranty
From Rs45/sq ft

Our Story

Today we are a team of 35 trained painters, 4 project managers and 2 interior consultants who have completed over 2,400 projects across Kolkata and its suburbs.
